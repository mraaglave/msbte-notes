Admission Notice for Direct Second Year Engineering FOR ACADEMIC YEAR 2023-24

Admission Notice for Diploma to Direct Second Year Engineering/Technology [3 Years Duration] for Academic Year 2023-24

This notice is for students wanting to join the second year of full-time undergraduate courses in Engineering and Technology (B.E. / B.Tech) for the academic year 2023-24.

Important Points

  • Online Registration: Register, scan, and upload documents online.
  • Verification: Choose E-Scrutiny (online verification) or Physical Scrutiny (visit a center).
  • Application Confirmation: Confirm your application online and pay the acceptance fee.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nationality: Must be an Indian National.
  • Diploma Holders: Completed a 3-year or 2-year (Lateral Entry) diploma in Engineering/Technology with at least 45% marks (40% for reserved categories) from a recognized institution.
  • B.Sc. Degree Holders: B.Sc. degree with at least 45% marks (40% for reserved categories) and Mathematics in HSC.
  • D.Voc. Stream: Passed D.Voc. in the same or allied sector.

Online Registration Process

  • Register online at www.mahacet.org.
  • Pay the application fee online using Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, or UPI.
  • General Category: ₹800, Reserved Category: ₹600.

Application Schedule

  • Registration Start: 13-07-2023
  • Registration End: 10-08-2023 (up to 05:00 PM)
  • Document Verification: 13-07-2023 to 11-08-2023 (up to 05:00 PM)
  • Merit List Display: 13-08-2023
  • Grievance Submission: 14-08-2023 to 17-08-2023 (up to 05:00 PM)
  • Final Merit List: 19-08-2023
  • CAP Round I: 20-08-2023 to 27-08-2023
  • CAP Round II: 29-08-2023 to 07-09-2023
  • CAP Round III: 09-09-2023 to 14-09-2023

Note: Applications registered after 04th August 2023 will be considered only for Non CAP Seats. Confirmations after 05th August 2023 will also be for Non CAP Seats.
