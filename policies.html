Policies and Guidelines

Our commitment at MSBTE Note and Information site is to provide accurate, timely, and relevant information to our students, teachers, and staff. All content published on this platform undergoes rigorous review to ensure quality and reliability.

  • Content Guidelines:
    • Ensure accuracy, relevance, and timeliness of information.
    • Prohibit misleading, discriminatory, offensive, or unlawful content.
    • Respect intellectual property rights and attribute sources appropriately.
  • User Conduct:
    • Maintain a respectful and inclusive environment.
    • Prohibit harassment, bullying, or abusive behavior.
    • Ban activities compromising site security (e.g., spamming, phishing).
  • Privacy Policy:
    • Transparently outline data collection, usage, and protection measures.
    • Provide users with control over their personal information.
    • Comply with data protection regulations and user privacy preferences.
  • Copyright and Permissions:
    • Uphold copyright laws and respect intellectual property rights.
    • Require proper attribution and permissions for content usage.
    • Provide mechanisms for reporting copyright infringements.
  • Accessibility:
    • Ensure the site is accessible to users with disabilities.
    • Comply with accessibility standards and provide alternative formats upon request.
    • Solicit feedback for continuous improvement of accessibility features.
  • Disclaimer:
    • Clarify limitations of information accuracy and liability.
    • Encourage independent verification of information.
    • Disclaim responsibility for user-generated content views.
  • Updates and Amendments:
    • Periodically review and update policies to reflect legal and operational changes.
    • Notify users of significant policy updates through prominent site notifications.
    • Continued use implies acceptance of revised policies.
  • Contact Information:
    • Provide contact details for inquiries, support, and reporting issues.
    • Ensure prompt response and assistance for user concerns.
