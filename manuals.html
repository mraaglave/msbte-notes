Disclaimer on How to Search

Welcome to our Manuals Selection Page. To help you find the right manual quickly and efficiently, please follow the guidelines below:

  1. Choose the Correct Manual Type:
    • Blank Manual: If you are looking for a blank manual to fill out yourself, select "Blank Manual".
    • Solved Manual: If you need a pre-solved manual, choose "Solved Manual".
  2. Select the Appropriate Scheme:
    • After choosing the manual type, select either "I-scheme" or "K-scheme" to get the specific manual for your curriculum.
  3. Accessing the Manuals:
    • Blank Manuals: Clicking the scheme will directly take you to the respective manual page.
    • Solved Manuals:
      • I-scheme: You will be redirected to a site where all solved manuals are available.
      • K-scheme: If it is currently unavailable, you will receive an alert notifying you of this.
  4. Tips for Effective Search:
    • Make sure to select the correct options from the dropdowns to avoid any navigation errors.
    • If a manual is not available, please check back later or contact our support for further assistance.

By following these steps, you can easily navigate through our selection process and find the manual you need. We strive to provide the most accurate and up-to-date resources for your convenience.
