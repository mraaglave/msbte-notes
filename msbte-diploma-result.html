MSBTE Diploma Result Summer 2024: Check Your Results Here

The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE) is set to announce the results for the Summer 2024 Diploma examinations by the last week of June 2024. Conducted for various courses including Engineering and Pharmacy, these exams were held in April/May 2024 across the state.

How to Check MSBTE Diploma Result Summer 2024:

  1. Visit the Official Website: Navigate to msbte.org.in.
  2. Find the Result Link: Look for the "MSBTE Result Summer 2024" link on the homepage.
  3. Enter Details: Provide your enrollment number or seat number as required.
  4. View Your Result: Click on "Show Result" to view your MSBTE Diploma Summer 2024 result.
  5. Save and Download: After checking your result, save and download the mark sheet for future reference.

MSBTE Diploma Marks Certificate Details:

The mark sheet will display your full name, enrollment number, seat number, year of examination, course name, subject details, total marks per subject, marks obtained, and overall result with pass percentage.

Pass Percentage Criteria: To qualify, candidates need a minimum of 40% marks (40 out of 100) in theory exams and at least 20 marks (out of 50) in practical exams for each subject.

About MSBTE:

Established by the Maharashtra government, MSBTE oversees diploma-level technical education across the state. Key initiatives include curriculum development, faculty and student development programs, and fostering industry-academic partnerships. Recently, MSBTE has expanded into skill development to enhance employability among diploma holders.

For the latest updates on MSBTE Diploma Result Summer 2024, candidates are advised to regularly visit the official MSBTE website. Stay tuned for the announcement expected in late June 2024.
